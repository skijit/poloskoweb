Polosko Custom Woodworks is proud to offer products constructed with environmental sensitivity through green design, sustainable materials, and non-toxic finishes.

Both Tony and Jamie are experienced in using local materials as well as Forest Stewardship Council sheet goods and hardwood. They are certified in Richlite fabrication and almost exclusively use non-toxic glues and finishes. Polosko is excited to offer customers the alternative of green construction to minimize the environmental impact of their work.

To read more about green construction, as well as the products Polosko uses, please visit these websites: Richlite, Paperstone, Plyboo, Forest Stewardship Council .
